Hi I am Xinyi. You can call me Philina (/phə-li-na/). I am a photographer, graphic designer, soccer fan, musical lover and cloudspotter. To me, sunset, starry night and snow are the most amazing view in the world. You can’t imagine how great nature is until you step into its heart. Even shapes and colors of a cloud are incredibly fascinating.

One of my goals is to combine photography with web design to generate interesting chemical reactions. To me, there are so many things that need to be learned whatever in Web Design or in Photography. But that’s why they are charming.

I am passionate about creating and capturing beautiful things. Also I have the ability to cooperate with other people and undertake multiple work and stress.
